Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Italian marble unique?

+
Italian marble, especially quarried from Carrara and Apuan Alps, possesses a signature crystalline matrix that offers high reflectivity, durability, and a deep luster that cannot be replicated by synthetic stones.

How does the AI scanning system help my project?

+
Our AI-native scanning captures high-fidelity digital twins of each slab. This system charts the exact coordinates of veins, allowing your architect to mock up, match, and align the slabs digitally before cutting begins.

Can we view the exact slabs before purchasing?

+
Yes. We display our blocks in full vertical format at our Mayapuri experience center. You can walk through our stock, select the exact slabs you want, and reserve them directly.

Do you export or supply outside Delhi NCR?

+
We regularly supply our premium stone collections to luxury villas, commercial projects, and hotels throughout India, ensuring secure, wood-crated logistics for safe transit.
